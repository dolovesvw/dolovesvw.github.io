About FreshLoaf

Our Story

I started baking in my small kitchen five years ago with a simple goal: to share good bread with my neighbors. What began as weekend experiments with natural starters became a passion for creating authentic artisan bread using traditional methods.

We use only four ingredients: organic flour, filtered water, sea salt, and time. No shortcuts, no preservatives, no dough conditioners. Each loaf is hand-shaped and baked in small batches to ensure the highest quality.

Our natural starter has been nurtured for over three years, giving our bread its distinctive tangy flavor and incredible texture. We source our flour from local mills and bake fresh every morning.
